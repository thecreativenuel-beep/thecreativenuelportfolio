Brand Carousel

Social Media Design • Instagram & X

Overview

This project focused on creating a visually consistent and engaging carousel design system for a creative brand, aimed at increasing engagement, enlightening audiences, and brand recognition across social platforms.

The Problem

The brand struggled with inconsistent visuals and low content retention. There was a need for a recognizable visual language that could scale across multiple posts without losing clarity.

The Solution

I designed a modular carousel system using bold typography, structured spacing, and a strong color hierarchy, allowing the brand to communicate complex ideas clearly and consistently.

Visuals

Outcome

The new design system improved audience engagement, strengthened brand recognition, and provided a reusable framework for future content.
