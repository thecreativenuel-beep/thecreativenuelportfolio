Brand Design 02

A modern brand system built with flexibility and emotional resonance in mind. This project explores identity adaptability across digital and physical platforms.

Case Study

AURORA is a modern activewear brand designed to inspire strength, motion, and confidence. The identity connects performance and style while remaining scalable across digital and physical platforms.

Outcome

The final visual system strengthened the brand’s presence across social and digital platforms, improved audience recognition, and introduced a flexible framework that scales with future growth.
